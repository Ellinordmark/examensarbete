Results

From the customers home to the closest facility, an average travel distance of 3.13 km was observed.

An average of 0.47 kg of CO₂ emissions per customer was observed, based on an average emission rate of 0.15 kg/km and the average travel distance of 3.13 km.

A total minimized weighted CO2 emissions of 21 924.83 Kg was observed.
A total minimized weighted distance of 146 165.53 Km was observed.

The total minimized travel distance and CO₂ emissions shown are based on the entire population within the selected area.

This is useful for large retailers (like IKEA) that serve a wide customer base and need to optimize a full store network. If you're a small business (like a local bakery), it's important to understand that not everyone in the region will visit your store.

The tool still helps compare potential locations, but the total values may overestimate real-world impact for smaller businesses. Always interpret the results in the context of your business size and target customers.
